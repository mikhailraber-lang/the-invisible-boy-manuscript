# 02_MANUSCRIPT

This folder contains all manuscript materials for The Invisible Boy.

---

# Manuscript Workflow Structure

## WORKING_CHATGPT
Active drafting, revision, and prose development work produced through ChatGPT collaboration.

## WORKING_CLAUDE
Structural, continuity, verification-preparation, and reconciliation work produced through Claude collaboration.

## RECONCILIATION
Comparison, merge, and canon reconciliation materials between competing manuscript versions.

## LOCKED
Finalized and verified canon chapters approved for locked repository state.

---

# Workflow Philosophy

The manuscript is the primary product.

Verification exists to protect:
- continuity
- canon integrity
- terminology consistency
- structural coherence

Verification does NOT replace:
- literary judgment
- emotional authenticity
- pacing decisions
- noir atmosphere
- prose quality

---

# Repository Rules

- Working drafts belong in WORKING folders.
- Merge review occurs in RECONCILIATION.
- Final approved chapters move into LOCKED.
- Locked chapters require explicit approval for revision.

Git history serves as the authoritative revision record.

---

# Automation Boundaries

Verification systems may assist:
- continuity review
- terminology enforcement
- timeline validation
- structural comparison

Verification systems may NOT:
- rewrite prose automatically
- alter pacing automatically
- replace literary judgment
- override emotional authenticity
