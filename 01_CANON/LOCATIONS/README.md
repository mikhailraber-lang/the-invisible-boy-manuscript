# Location Canon

This directory contains canonical environmental and spatial continuity references for *The Invisible Boy*.

Location canon exists to preserve:
- environmental consistency
- spatial continuity
- noir atmosphere
- recurring setting integrity
- investigative geography
- sensory continuity

---

# Location Standards

Location materials may include:

- recurring locations
- environmental descriptions
- building layouts
- atmospheric references
- sensory details
- spatial relationships
- access routes
- investigative relevance
- continuity restrictions

Descriptions should remain concise, atmospheric, and continuity-focused.

---

# Repository Rules

Major locations should remain structurally and atmospherically consistent across approved materials.

Major revisions should undergo:
- continuity review
- reconciliation review if necessary
- author approval before LOCKED status

Locked environmental references may not be altered without explicit approval.

---

# AI Interaction Rules

AI systems may:
- reference approved locations
- validate environmental continuity
- detect spatial contradictions
- compare atmospheric consistency

AI systems may NOT:
- redesign canonical environments automatically
- mutate approved spatial relationships
- override noir atmosphere guidance
- reinterpret locked location references

---

# Canon Priority

When conflicts occur:

1. LOCKED location canon
2. APPROVED canon
3. VERIFIED continuity materials
4. Draft manuscripts
5. Experimental AI outputs

# Literary Principle

Locations support atmosphere.

Atmosphere remains literary, not procedural.
