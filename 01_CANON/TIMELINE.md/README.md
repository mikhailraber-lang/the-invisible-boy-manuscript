# Timeline Canon

This directory contains canonical chronological continuity for *The Invisible Boy*.

Timeline canon exists to preserve:
- chronological consistency
- investigative sequencing
- event causality
- narrative progression
- temporal continuity
- continuity verification integrity

---

# Timeline Standards

Timeline materials may include:

- master chronology
- event sequencing
- chapter-to-time mapping
- flashback positioning
- investigative milestones
- character movement tracking
- temporal dependency references
- continuity anchors
- unresolved timeline conflicts

Timeline records should remain concise, structured, and verification-oriented.

---

# Repository Rules

Timeline continuity must remain internally consistent across all approved materials.

Major revisions should undergo:
- continuity review
- reconciliation review if necessary
- author approval before LOCKED status

Locked timeline anchors may not be altered without explicit approval.

---

# AI Interaction Rules

AI systems may:
- validate chronological consistency
- detect temporal conflicts
- compare sequencing logic
- identify continuity gaps

AI systems may NOT:
- silently rewrite chronology
- alter locked timeline anchors
- reinterpret established event order automatically
- override approved continuity decisions

---

# Canon Priority

When conflicts occur:

1. LOCKED timeline canon
2. APPROVED canon
3. VERIFIED continuity materials
4. Draft manuscripts
5. Experimental AI outputs

# Literary Principle

Chronology supports narrative clarity.

Narrative pacing remains a literary judgment.
