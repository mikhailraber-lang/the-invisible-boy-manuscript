# 01_CANON

## Purpose

This folder contains the canonical continuity layer for *The Invisible Boy*.

It is the **source of truth** for all character, terminology, plot, location, timeline, and relationship rules across every AI collaborator and every writing session.

---

## Structure

This folder remains **flat by design.** One file, one source of truth:

```
01_CANON/
└── MASTER_CHEAT_SHEET.md
```

No subfolders. No fragmented canon files. No modular hierarchy.

---

## Why Flat

The unified `MASTER_CHEAT_SHEET.md` is the:

- Primary continuity restoration document
- Fast-load context anchor for every AI session
- Operational canon reference layer
- Anti-drift authority for terminology, characters, and rules

Fragmenting canon into multiple files would create:

- Synchronization risk (canon drifting between files)
- Slower session onboarding (multiple uploads instead of one)
- Maintenance burden (multiple files to update on every canon change)
- Contradiction risk between fragmented sources

The locked architectural decision (May 25, 2026):

> *"Modular decomposition is postponed unless token limits become problematic, continuity density becomes operationally unmanageable, or maintenance burden materially increases."*

None of those conditions exist. Therefore: one file.

---

## When to Use This Folder

**At the start of every AI session:**
Upload `MASTER_CHEAT_SHEET.md` to restore full project context.

**When canon changes are approved by Misha:**
Update `MASTER_CHEAT_SHEET.md` directly. Commit with a clear message.

**Never:**
Create alternate canon files, parallel rule documents, or supplementary canon hierarchies without Misha's explicit instruction.

---

## Authority

`MASTER_CHEAT_SHEET.md` is the operational canon reference, not the final authority.

**Misha is the final authority.** The cheat sheet records Misha's decisions; it does not make them.
