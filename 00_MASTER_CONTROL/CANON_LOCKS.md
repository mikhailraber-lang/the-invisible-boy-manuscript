# CANON_LOCKS

Locked canon rules for The Invisible Boy project.

## Locked Rules

1. Maksim is always spelled "Maksim"
2. Use "the OD case" terminology consistently
3. "The Ghost" replaces deprecated terminology
4. Steve "cared for" Maksim
5. Thomas Vance is not the training officer
6. Maksim is revealed through fragments only
7. Noir authenticity must be preserved
8. Verification supports storytelling, not vice versa
9. Human literary judgment overrides advisory systems
10. Locked chapters require explicit approval for revision

## Canon Protection Principles

- Canon integrity takes priority over workflow convenience.
- Verification systems are advisory unless hard canon violations occur.
- No automated system may silently alter literary canon.
- GitHub is the source of repository truth.
