# REPOSITORY_RULES

## Repository Principles

1. The manuscript is the primary product.
2. Verification supports storytelling but does not replace storytelling.
3. Mechanical consistency may be automated.
4. Literary judgment remains human-directed.
5. Locked canon may not be casually modified.
6. Git history is the source of revision tracking.
7. GitHub is the repository source of truth.
8. No automated system may silently alter literary canon.
9. Reconciliation occurs before canon locking.
10. Main branch contains verified material only.

---

## Workflow States

- DRAFT
- UNDER_REVISION
- VERIFIED
- LOCKED

---

## Branch Structure

### main
Verified and locked material only.

### working
Active drafting, revisions, and reconciliation work.

---

## Human Authority

Final creative and canon authority belongs to Misha.

Verification systems are advisory unless:
- hard canon conflicts occur
- timeline impossibilities occur
- locked terminology violations occur
