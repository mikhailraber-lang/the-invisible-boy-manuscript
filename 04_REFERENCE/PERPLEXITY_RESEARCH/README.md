# PERPLEXITY_RESEARCH

## Purpose

This folder contains research, fact verification, procedural reference material, and environmental realism support sourced through Perplexity sessions for *The Invisible Boy*.

Perplexity contributes external research that helps ground the novel's investigative, procedural, technical, geographic, and social realism.

Perplexity output is reference-support material that informs the manuscript — not the manuscript itself.

# Integration Philosophy

Perplexity research informs the manuscript but does not dictate it.

Workflow:

1. A procedural, technical, legal, forensic, or environmental question arises
2. Perplexity performs external research
3. Research output is stored in `PERPLEXITY_RESEARCH/`
4. Manuscript-development AIs and human review reference the material when drafting or reconciling
5. Misha determines how the findings translate into narrative prose

Research is NEVER automatically incorporated into canon or manuscript text.

The author always mediates between:
- realism
- pacing
- noir atmosphere
- emotional structure
- narrative psychology

Research realism should support noir atmosphere and narrative psychology — not override them.

---

# Research Reliability Rule

External research outputs must still be independently evaluated for:

- accuracy
- consistency
- source quality
- legal nuance
- procedural applicability
- manuscript relevance

Research findings are advisory support material, not automatic truth.

Perplexity may still:
- hallucinate
- oversimplify
- misinterpret procedural nuance
- produce false certainty

Human review remains mandatory.

# Realism Anchor

*The Invisible Boy* incorporates realism inspired by real-world investigative, social, technical, and psychological dynamics.

Perplexity research exists to:

- validate procedural details against real-world practice
- catch unrealistic shortcuts
- prevent Hollywood-style investigative logic
- verify technical limitations and capabilities
- ground geographic/environmental details
- improve social and procedural authenticity
- support investigative credibility

Narrative needs may still require selective compression or adaptation.
